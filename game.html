What makes Good Game Design



A lot of modern AAA game titles miss the mark on what makes a good game.
However some games in the modern era figure it out and as such become classics.

There are 3 key elements to good game design


1. Mechanics that work well with the style and feel of the game
2. Levels or encounters that push you into said mechanics
3. A sense of player progression that happens as the game goes on
